[PS 110:6]
VIRTŪTEM OPERUM SUŌRUM ANNUNCIĀBIT POPULŌ SUŌ

[PS 110:7]

[PS 110:8]

[PS 110:9]

[PS 111:1]

[PS 111:2]
POTĒNS IN TERRĀ ERIT SĒMEN EIUS GENERĀTIŌ RĒCTŌRUM BENEDĪCĒTUR 

[PS 111:3]

[PS 111:6]
QUIA IN ÆTERNUM NŌN COMMOVĒBITUR 

[PS 111:7]

[PS 111:10]
