Purpose

This document provides a Psalm–Plate index for the manuscript Paris, Bibliothèque nationale de France, nouvelles acquisitions latines 442 (the Tironian Psalter).

The index is intended as a navigational aid for consultation of the manuscript. It identifies which Psalms appear on which manuscript plates, using stable plate identifiers.

This document asserts location only.
It makes no interpretive, editorial, or textual claims.


---

Scope and conventions

Psalm numbering follows the Latin Psalter tradition (Vulgate/LXX).

Plate identifiers (e.g., PLATE-0XX) refer to fixed manuscript surfaces used throughout the associated project.

Where a plate contains more than one Psalm, ranges are indicated.

Verse-level boundaries are not asserted unless explicitly known.

The index is independent of transcription completeness or editorial state.
