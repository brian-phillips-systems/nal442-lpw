[Ps 96:2]

[Ps 96:4]

[Ps 96:6]

[Ps 96:7]
CŌNFUNDANTUR OMNĒS QUĪ ADŌRANT SCULPTILIA ET QUĪ GLŌRIANTUR IN SIMULĀCRĪS SUĪS ADŌRĀTE EUM OMNĒS ANGELĪ EIUS

[Ps 96:8]

[Ps 97:1]
PSALMUS IPSĪ DĀVĪD CANTĀTĒ DOMINŌ CANTICUM NOVUM QUIA MĪRĀBILIA FĒCIT SALVĀVIT SIBI DEXTERĀ EIUS ET BRĀCHIUM SĀNCTUM EIUS
